AndyBucks

My own personal currency backed by Bitcoin. Each AndyBuck has its own address on the blockchain containing $1 worth of Bitcoin (at the time of printing).

A QR code representing the public key associated with the address is printed on the front. The private key is printed underneath a scratch off adhesive so that you can 'redeem' your Bitcoin at any time.

(I've since taken it down because I'm not maintaining it)
